About Our Department

The Department of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AIML) was established in 2022. Our program focuses on developing the next generation of AI professionals who can contribute to technological advancement.

We emphasize both theoretical knowledge and practical application, ensuring our students are well-prepared for the industry or further research.

Highlights

  • State-of-the-art computing labs
  • Industry-focused curriculum
  • Regular workshops and seminars
  • Collaboration with leading tech companies
  • Research opportunities for students
  • Expert faculty with industry experience
